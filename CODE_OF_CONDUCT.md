# Code of Conduct

This project adheres to the Contributor Covenant v2.1.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity/expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Showing empathy and respect.
- Giving/accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers may clarify, remove, or reject contributions not aligned with this Code.

## Scope
Applies in all project spaces and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement
Report abuse to the maintainers via private channel (see SECURITY.md). Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Guidelines
Enforcement will follow a graduated response (informal warning → temporary restriction → ban) based on severity and recurrence.
